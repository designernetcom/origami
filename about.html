About Us


We are manufacturing of Printing, Packaging, Customize Machine

Our director, Mr. Nitin Handedeshmukh, established Origami Machines Pvt. Ltd. in the year 1999 in Pune, Maharashtra. Since more than 23 years ago, the business has offered design and production services for printing and packaging equipment.

We are ISO 9001 : 2015 Cetified.

Origami Machines Pvt. ltd. is a fast-growing solution provider company in the segments of PHARMA, FOOD , FMCG & Beverage, Cosmetics. Our Company is committed to meet the new customer requirement along with total satisfaction since 1999.

We are proud manufacturer and suppliers of following Packaging and Printing products :

  • Labelling Machines
