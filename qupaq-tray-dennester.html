QUPAQ Tray Dennester



QUPAQ Tray Dennester

The ANYTRAY line of pneumatic denesters is the go-to choice for food manufacturers in need of advanced denester technology. With its ability to handle up to 120 trays per minute, these denesters are designed to improve production flow and increase efficiency for food technology operations of all sizes. The ANYTRAY denesters are known for their low maintenance and service requirements, making it an easy choice for food manufacturing companies looking to automate their production process without adding dedicated expertise. Additionally, all denesters in the ANYTRAY line are built with a focus on no-compromise hygiene, ensuring reliable uptime for our customers.

Technical Specifications :

Length 360mm
Width 280mm
Height 1300 mm
Weight 350 kg
Band Widths 120 trays per minute

Applications :

  • Tray denesting and dispensing
  • Dispenses and stacks trays, boxes, food packaging, containers, cups, plastic pots, and so much more
  • Numerous tray shapes and sizes including: rectangular; square; round; oval; octagon – any shapes available in the marketplace
  • Tray compositions supported include: aluminum; foam; surgical plastic trays; plastic “tubs”; paperboard; and much more
